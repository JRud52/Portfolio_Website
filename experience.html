Game Events

Global Game Jam

2012 - 2014
While not techinically a competition, I have participated in every Global Game Jam that I was able to since I found out about its existance. A good mix of 2D and 3D games have come out of this event.

Sudbury Game Design Compeition

An event that started last year, the Sudbury Game Design Competition brought in 15 teams in a Game Jam style of competition. The end products were judged by a panel of judges, and our game was awarded third place.

Protolaunch

An event that I was able to participate in for its final year, Protolaunch was a series of workshops that were run on several weekends during the year, that brought in industry professionals to do a talk and then an activity with the participants. The summer event was the best part though. We were split up into teams of 4 and we each had 5 weeks to make the best game prototype that we possibly could make. At the end of the competition, the games were judged, and my team was awarded with second place.
